## Vision

### 10x Check
CadenceSprout is not just a better ERP — it's an **AI-native early childhood OS**. Teachers take a 10-second video, AI identifies children, tags developmental milestones, drafts parent captions in the parent's language. Parents get personalized daily summaries. At year-end, AI generates a full developmental portfolio for every child automatically. School owners see real-time engagement scores per school. The product learns from 200+ schools' milestone data and becomes India's most accurate early childhood development signal — a data asset no competitor can replicate.

### Platonic Ideal
A teacher opens CadenceSprout at 9am. She takes a 15-second video of the morning circle. The app surfaces: "3 milestones detected: Rohan — turn-taking; Priya — verbal expression; Kabir — number recognition. Post to parents?" She taps Send. 47 parents see a beautiful, branded update in their feed (and in WhatsApp if they haven't installed the app yet). One parent shares it to their family group. Three relatives ask "which school is this?" That's the product. Every day. Every class. Every school.

## Team Assumption

**Phase 1 team:** 4-5 engineers (1 senior backend, 1 React Native, 1 Next.js/frontend, 1 AI/ML, 1 junior fullstack) + 1 product/design. This is the headcount the 10-month timeline is calibrated against. If team is smaller, timeline stretches proportionally.

## Phase 1 Launch Criteria (Month 10)

- 1 preschool chain (5+ centers) fully live, daily active teachers and parents
- ≥70% of enrolled parents using the feed (active = viewed a post in last 7 days)
- ≥3 posts per class per day by teachers
- Self-serve onboarding live: a new school can sign up and invite teachers without founder involvement
- At least 3 schools live on manual invoicing (Razorpay auto-billing is Phase 2)
- NPS from teachers ≥ 30

## Pricing Decision

**Per-child pricing — launch rate: ₹20–₹30/child/month** (revised down from ₹50-100 after outside voice challenge). At 50 children per school: ₹1,000–₹1,500/school/month = ₹12K–₹18K/school/year. This is below Illumine's ₹800-1,500/student/year on a per-student basis but priced to get standalone preschool chains (₹0 current spend) to their first paid commitment. **Price increase to ₹40-60/child/month planned at Month 12 once 10+ schools are live and NPS ≥ 30.** Must be resolved before Month 2 Razorpay integration.

- New backend from scratch (no ERP reuse)
- **English-first at launch; Hindi + Tamil deferred to Month 12 / post-PMF** (removed from Phase 1 to protect Month 5-8 build capacity)

## Key Architectural Decisions

1. **Build from scratch** — no reuse from Cadence Infotech's existing ERP. New backend, new mobile apps, new web dashboard.
2. **Per child per month pricing** — ₹20-30/child/month (launch rate). Year 1 billing: manual invoicing. Razorpay auto-billing in Phase 2 (post product completion). Fraud mitigation deferred to Phase 2 when auto-billing is implemented.
3. **WhatsApp magic-link** — progressive engagement: Wapi sends WhatsApp message → link opens web view → after 3 engagements, prompt native app install. Risk: Wapi is an unofficial gateway; reliability is ~95% uptime, can be blocked. Fallback: SMS via Twilio/MSG91 if Wapi fails. Migrate to official WhatsApp Business API post-launch.
4. **AI caption drafting + milestone suggestion** — Claude API (claude-haiku-4-5 for cost, claude-sonnet-4-6 for quality). Teacher reviews AI draft before posting — never auto-posts.
5. **Child identification in photos/videos** — AWS Rekognition server-side for face recognition (ML Kit only does face *detection*, not *recognition* — these are different). Class roster photos uploaded at enrollment as reference embeddings. Photos sent to Rekognition only after explicit DPDP consent. Results returned as suggested child tags; teacher confirms before posting. Added cost: ~$0.001/image at launch scale. DPDP explicit consent screen required at parent onboarding and covers server-side processing.
6. **NEP 2020 milestone taxonomy** — Use the NCERT Foundational Stage Curriculum (FSC) document (public, 2022) as the milestone taxonomy source. Pre-load ~80 developmental indicators across 5 domains (physical, cognitive, language, social-emotional, aesthetic). This taxonomy must be defined and loaded before AI tagging is implemented (Month 3). Assign ownership to product lead.
7. **Engagement score formula** — School-level: (parents who viewed ≥1 post this week / total enrolled parents) × 100. Simple, transparent, easy to explain to school owners. Stored as a weekly snapshot, not real-time.
