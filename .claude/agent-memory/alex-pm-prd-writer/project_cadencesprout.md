CadenceSprout is an All-in-One Preschool OS for India. Primary ICP: standalone preschool chain owners running 2-10 centers with no existing ERP.

**Phase 1 scope (Month 0-10):**
- Seesaw Layer: parent feed, AI activity tagging (AWS Rekognition + OpenRouter), NEP 2020 milestone tracker, WhatsApp magic-link onboarding, school-level engagement score dashboard, AI year-end portfolio
- ERP Layer: admissions, manual fee invoicing, attendance, staff management (names/roles/class only), self-serve school onboarding
- English-only at launch

**Pricing:** ₹20-30/child/month at launch → ₹40-60/child/month at Month 12 (when 10+ schools live and NPS ≥ 30)

**Launch criteria (Month 10):** 1 chain (5+ centers) fully live, ≥70% parent adoption, ≥3 posts/class/day, self-serve onboarding live, ≥3 schools on manual invoicing, teacher NPS ≥ 30

**Build from scratch:** No reuse of Cadence Infotech's existing ERP

**Key competitors:** Illumine ($2.5M seed, 3,000+ centers, English-first, going wide); CadenceSprout bets on India depth, design quality, NEP 2020 alignment

**Pilot school:** Secured via existing Cadence Infotech contact — free for first 3 months

**Why:** Engagement score = (parents who viewed ≥1 post in trailing 7 days ÷ total enrolled parents) × 100, stored as weekly snapshot

**How to apply:** Always frame feature decisions against the primary ICP (standalone preschool chains, no existing ERP, WhatsApp-dependent). Reference Phase 1 launch criteria when scoping.
